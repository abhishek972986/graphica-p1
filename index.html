    Competitive Programming Club is one of the biggest clubs of AIT Pune. It provides a supportive environment for beginner and experienced coders

    GDSC offers practical experience in coding, app development, and emerging technologies, complementing theoretical knowledge.

  • NSS

    Social dev New

    NSS fosters a sense of civic duty by encouraging students to engage in community service and social welfare activities. Working on community projects enhances teamwork and collaboration, essential skills for any professional environment.

    Festival org.

    Spiritual clubs provide a space for self-reflection, helping students explore their values, beliefs, and purpose. These clubs foster a sense of belonging and support, connecting students who share similar spiritual interests or practices.
